POKEHUNTER

You spot a desirable pokemon in your sightings list. But how can you narrow down it's location?

Tap "Sighted!" to add a green circle when the pokemon is in your sightings list.

Tap "Out of sight" to add a red circle if it disappears.

The pokemon will be in the darkest green area -- unless it despawns!

Remember your sightings list in Pokemon Go! only updates every 10 seconds or so.
